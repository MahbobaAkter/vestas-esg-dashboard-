Insights

Vestas demonstrates strong ESG performance, particularly in the environmental dimension, as its core business directly supports renewable energy generation. The company’s efforts in low-emission materials and circularity further strengthen its sustainability position.

While environmental impact remains the strongest pillar, continuous improvements in workforce safety and governance transparency indicate a balanced and evolving ESG strategy.
